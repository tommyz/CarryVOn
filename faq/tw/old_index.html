How to download video?

To download a video, please in Home search and select the video. Then upper right corner can touch it. Start download the video.

Why sometime download video show advertising?

Because one day have twice times can not show advertising download video, if you want more download video on one day, you will see advertising

What is the download limit?

You can save up to 20 videos. If you have used up to all 20 video, you maybe delete some video to creat space for new video

Why can't I download Youtube videos?

The website's term of service prohibits video from downloaded on their website.

背景播放控制器無法上一首下一首

請將App關閉重開，不要在首頁點擊任何影片。

為何不能下載影片

可能是影片檔案大小解析錯誤

為何不能下載直播影片

因為直播影片並沒有一個完整的檔案所以無法下載

為何會出現沒有免費廣告請稍候下載

建議稍後再試，或是升級為去廣告版。

為何下載影片速度很慢

可能是裝置使用的網路速度原因，建議在網路訊號良好的地方下載影片。
